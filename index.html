Frequently Asked Questions

What are the key specifications of the MacBook Air M2?
The MacBook Air M2 features an Apple M2 chip, 8GB/16GB RAM options, 256GB/512GB/1TB SSD storage, a 13.3-inch Retina display, and macOS.
How long does the MacBook Air M2's battery last?
The MacBook Air M2 offers up to 15 hours of battery life on a single charge for all-day productivity and entertainment.
Is the RAM upgradable on the MacBook Air M2?
No, the RAM on the MacBook Air M2 is not upgradable. Choose your preferred RAM option when purchasing.
What operating system does the MacBook Air M2 use?
The MacBook Air M2 runs on macOS, Apple's user-friendly and secure operating system.
